Introduction

This package implements confounding robust estimators discussed in Ishikawa, et. al. (2023) [2] and its preliminary version [1]. These estimators robustifies the classical inverse probability weighting estimator, by considering the worst-case confounding and provide lower bound (or upper bound) of the policy value.

The supported estimators includes: inverse probability weighting (IPW) estimator, Hajek estimator, kernel conditional moment constraint (KCMC) estimator, and quantile balancing (QB) estimator. For the exhausive list of supported estimators, see confounding_robust_inference.estimators.
